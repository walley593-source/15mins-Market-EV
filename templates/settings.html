Credentials & Live Trading

Live mode places real Fill-Or-Kill market orders on the Polymarket CLOB. The wallet must hold USDC on Polygon and have approved the exchange contracts. Leave Funder blank for an EOA (type 0).

One-time: approves the Polymarket contracts to spend this wallet's USDC/CTF. Save your private key first; needs a little POL for gas. Not needed for proxy wallets.
